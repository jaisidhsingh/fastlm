## Technical stuff

### How do we study GBS & LR at different scales?

**Parameter scales (10M, 20M, 50M, 150M, 300M for val):**
We fix the world size (WS) to 4 (5M & 50M) or 8 (150M & 300M) and max out MBS. Then we use GAS to vary GBS. Then for each GBS we sweep over an LR grid, *however,* we only do this for (i) pure attention, (ii) pure GDN. Depending on how close the extracted power laws for GBS and learning rate are, we will select one final trend to represent these hyper-parameter trends for all architectures.

#### Economics

- GAS sweep: [1, 4, 8, 16]
- LR sweep: [1/4096, 1/1024, 1/256, 1/128, 1/64]
- Runs per architecture per scale = 4 x 5
- Total runs per scale = 2 x 4 x 5 = 40
- Training time in hours
    - 10M: 40 x 0.22 = 8.8 → 2.2 on WS=4
    - 20M: 40 x 0.62 = 24.8 → 6.2 on WS=4
    - 50M: 40 x 2.62 = 104.8 → 26.2 (1 day) on WS=4
    - 150M: 40 x 11.57 = 462.8 → 57.8 (2.5 days) on WS=8
    - 300M: 40 x 45.04 = 1,801.6 → 225.2 (9.4 days) on WS=8
- Total GPU hours $\approx$ 2400
- If we include 1:3 hybrid as well then we get a total of 3600 GPU hours
